Student Loans Company provides loans to students active during this pandemic in Africa, Asia and Latin America. We work with partner organisations which aim to contribute to improving the lives of low-income stdents and communities.

SLC seeks to provide loans of between $ 500 and $ 2,000 to students active in the financial inclusion, agriculture and renewable energy sectors in 33 focus countries across our three regions. Our loans typically mature in 24 hours.

We look for partners that:
  • create jobs and income for students
  • financial institutions or small to medium enterprises
  • have women in influential positions such as in management
  • are environmentally sustainable and respect animal welfare
  • have suitable management in place and are (or can soon become) financially sustainable
  • have a clear need for foreign investment
